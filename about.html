About KothaKhoj

Connecting room providers and seekers directly in Nepal.

Our Story

KothaKhoj was born out of a simple frustration: finding a good room in Kathmandu was unnecessarily complicated and expensive due to broker fees and lack of transparency.

Founded in 2025 by two friends, Himal and Ishwor, KothaKhoj aims to revolutionize the room rental process in Nepal by directly connecting those offering rooms with those searching for them.

Our name "KothaKhoj" combines the Nepali words "Kotha" (room) and "Khoj" (search), reflecting our mission to simplify the room-hunting process for everyone in Nepal.

Our Mission

At KothaKhoj, we believe finding a place to live should be simple, transparent, and accessible. Our mission is to build a user-friendly platform that connects room seekers with a wide range of available rooms, whether listed by individuals or those with more options to offer.

Direct Connections

We make room searching easier by directly connecting those looking for a place to stay with those offering rooms. Whether you're an individual renting out a space or someone searching for the perfect room, our platform ensures smooth and hassle-free communication.

Transparency

We promote honest listings with accurate information and real photos to build trust.

Local Focus

We understand the unique needs of people across Nepal and tailor our platform accordingly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is KothaKhoj completely free to use?

Yes, KothaKhoj is completely free for both room owners and room seekers. We do not charge any commission or fees for connecting you.

How do I know the listings are genuine?

We encourage owners to upload real photos and provide accurate information. We also have a verification process for listings and allow users to report any suspicious listings.

Can I list multiple properties on KothaKhoj?

Yes, property owners can list multiple rooms or properties on our platform. Each listing should be created separately with its own details and photos.

How do I contact a room owner?

When you find a room you're interested in, you can view the owner's contact information on the room details page. You can contact them directly via phone, WhatsApp, or email as provided by the owner.

How long does it take for my listing to appear?

Most listings appear immediately after submission. However, we do perform basic verification checks which might take up to 24 hours in some cases.

Can I save rooms to view later?

Yes, you can save rooms to your favorites list by clicking the heart icon on any room listing. You can access your saved rooms from the listings page.
